DESIGN SYSTEMS

Leia 3D Design System

Brand refresh and design system for lightfield displays

Role: Senior Visual Designer (Full-time) | Timeline: Aug 2021 – Jan 2023

Skills: Design systems, brand design, 3D design, Figma, cross-functional collaboration

OVERVIEW

Leia Inc creates 3D lightfield display technology—screens that show depth without glasses. When I joined, the company had fragmented visual language across 10+ apps and services, no systematic approach to designing for 3D, and no shared component library.

I led the brand refresh and built the design system from 0→1, establishing the visual language, component architecture, and design principles adopted by 50+ designers and engineers across the organization.

THE CHALLENGE

How do you create a design system for a technology that has no existing design patterns?

  • Lightfield displays show depth—every component needs to work in 3D space, not just 2D
  • 10+ apps built by different teams with different visual languages and no consistency
  • No industry precedent—we were defining what "good 3D UI design" means
  • Cross-functional complexity: 50+ people across design, engineering, and product teams

MY APPROACH

I broke this into five phases:

  1. Brand Foundation - Refresh the visual identity to create a strong foundation
  2. 3D Design Principles - Define how to design for lightfield displays
  3. Component System - Build a comprehensive, flexible component library
  4. Documentation & Adoption - Ensure teams could actually use the system
  5. Implementation & Governance - Collaborate with engineering and establish ongoing maintenance

IMPACT

  • 50+ designers and engineers using the system daily
  • 10+ apps and services with unified visual language
  • Reduced design-to-dev handoff time by ~40%
  • First comprehensive design system for lightfield display technology
  • System still in active use 2+ years after launch
