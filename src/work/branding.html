    Orosa Beauty

  • Complete brand identity system and visual language
  • Packaging architecture and seasonal product systems
  • Art direction, photography, and motion graphics

I built the complete brand identity system for Orosa Beauty, a vegan nail care brand targeting beauty-obsessed consumers who find inspiration in color, delight, and play. The visual language needed to flex across seasonal product launches, packaging touchpoints, social content, and retail presence while maintaining a cohesive brand voice.

The system included modular design components—typography, color palettes, graphic patterns, and motion principles—that allowed the brand to scale across new product lines without requiring redesign from scratch each season.

    Vesca Beauty

  • Brand identity system and packaging architecture
  • Art direction and product photography
  • Visual language built around golden hour warmth

I developed the brand identity and visual system for Vesca Beauty, a bronzer line inspired by golden hour warmth and destination aesthetics. The brand concept—"catch Vesca at golden hour, chasing the sun till dusk"—drove every design decision, from packaging architecture to photography art direction.

The visual language needed to evoke sensorial warmth and wanderlust while functioning across diverse touchpoints: retail packaging, PR experiences, shipping materials, and social content. Each element reinforced the brand's core positioning around radiant, universally flattering products.
