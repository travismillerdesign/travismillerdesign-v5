My role

Apple.com serves millions of daily visitors across dozens of product teams. My role focused on architecting core components for the design system, ensuring accessibility and performance through engineering collaboration, and creating documentation that serves designers, developers, and stakeholders.

Component Architecture

Core components designed to work across 20+ product teams. Each component balances flexibility for diverse use cases with constraints that maintain consistency at scale.
