Scotiabank Arena

The animated graphics for Scotiabank Arena's visual identity display externally as an ambient feature of the stadium in Toronto's downtown core. Seen by millions of annual visitors and residents, the motion system remains in use today.

Visual System

Created motion graphics designed for large-scale LED displays on a landmark building. The animation system needed to work across varying viewing distances and lighting conditions while maintaining the Scotiabank Arena brand identity.

{% responsiveImage "/assets/motion-design/scotiabankarena/scotiabankarena_exteriormockup.jpg", "Scotiabank Arena exterior featuring animated LED displays showing the motion graphics system on the building facade", "fillImage" %}
Impact

The motion graphics have been displayed continuously since the rebrand, reaching millions of annual viewers in one of Toronto's highest-traffic areas. Working with Creative Director Amin Todai and Art Director Jeff Rae at OneMethod.
