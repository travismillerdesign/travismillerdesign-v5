### Section 1: Design Systems

#### Project: Leia Inc. Lume Pad 2 - Design System
**ID:** `leia`
**Image directory:** `assets/leia/`

**Hero Image:** `assets/leia/Leia_Wallpaper_1.jpg`

**Project Header:**
- **Title:** Leia Inc Lume Pad 2 Design System
- **Role:** Senior Visual Designer

**Project Highlights (Bullet Points):**
- Established 0 to 1 design system adopted by 50+ designers and engineers across 10+ apps
- Led brand refresh that unified visual language and became foundation for product design
- Reduced design-to-dev handoff time by 40% through systematic component architecture
- Successfully launched with Lume Pad 2 after 18 months of cross-functional collaboration
- Created first systematic approach to designing for lightfield technology with no industry precedent

**Content Sections:**

**The Challenge**
Leia needed a design system for lightfield display technology where no systematic approach existed. The company had grown rapidly without unified design language, creating inconsistency across 10+ apps and making it difficult for new designers to contribute effectively.

**My Approach**
I led the creation of a comprehensive design system from scratch, starting with a brand refresh that would establish the visual foundation. I collaborated with 50+ designers and engineers to define components, patterns, and guidelines specific to 3D spatial interfaces.

**The Outcome**
The system became the foundation for all Leia products, reducing design-to-dev time by 40% and enabling rapid scaling of the design team. It launched with the Lume Pad 2 and continues to evolve as the product line grows.

#### Project: Design Systems at Scale
**ID:** `design-systems-scaled`
**Image directory:** `assets/design-systems-scaled/`

**Project Header:**
- **Title:** Design Systems at Scale
- **Role:** Senior Designer / Design Systems Designer
- **Timeline:** 2021 - Present
- **Companies:** Apple, Google

**Project Highlights:**
- **Apple:** Architected core components for apple.com's design system used by 20+ product teams serving millions of daily visitors
- **Google Project Starline:** Built foundational design system for 3D spatial video communication, defining UX patterns and motion guidelines
- Ensured accessibility (WCAG AA) and performance through engineering collaboration and code prototyping
- Created documentation systems for multiple audiences: designers, developers, researchers, stakeholders
- Established systematic handoff processes that reduced development friction

**Content Sections:**

**Working at Enterprise Scale**
Building design systems for products used by millions requires different thinking than greenfield projects. I've worked on both sides: defining patterns within mature systems at Apple and building foundational systems for emerging technology at Google.

**Process & Governance**
My approach focuses on creating systems that teams actually adopt. This means thorough documentation, clear governance models, and close collaboration with engineering to ensure components are performant and accessible.

**Impact**
The systems I've built serve 20+ product teams and millions of daily visitors. By focusing on adoption and maintainability, I create infrastructure that scales with the product.
